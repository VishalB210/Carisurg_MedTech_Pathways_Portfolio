**Week 6 Final - Baseline Model Confusion Matrices**

I compared logistic regression and a bounded decision tree for predicting ESI triage level. Logistic regression performed better overall, but it correctly identified only 4 of 16 true ESI Level 1 cases. The decision tree identified only 1 of 16. This shows why overall accuracy alone is not enough for an imbalanced triage problem and why detection of the most urgent patients must be reviewed separately.
